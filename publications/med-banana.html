Abstract摘要

Recent advances in multimodal large language models have enabled remarkable medical image editing capabilities. However, progress in the research community remains constrained by the absence of large-scale, high-quality, and openly accessible datasets built specifically for medical image editing. We introduce Med-Banana-50K, a comprehensive 50K-image dataset for instruction-based medical image editing spanning three modalities (chest X-ray, brain MRI, fundus photography) and 23 disease types. Our dataset employs LLM-as-Judge with medically grounded rubric and history-aware iterative refinement for quality control, ensuring high-quality medical image edits that preserve anatomical accuracy while achieving the desired modifications.

多模态大语言模型的进步使医学图像编辑能力显著提升。然而，研究社区的进展受限于缺乏大规模、高质量、开放获取的医学图像编辑专用数据集。我们提出 Med-Banana-50K，一个包含 5 万张图像的综合性指令式医学图像编辑数据集，涵盖三种模态（胸部 X 光、脑 MRI、眼底照相）和 23 种疾病类型。数据集采用基于医学评分准则的 LLM-as-Judge 和历史感知迭代优化进行质量控制，确保高质量医学图像编辑在保持解剖准确性的同时实现期望的修改。

Key Features:关键特性：

  • Bidirectional Editing:双向编辑： Supports both lesion addition and removal, enabling comprehensive medical image editing research. 支持病灶添加和移除，实现全面的医学图像编辑研究。
  • Multi-Modality Coverage:多模态覆盖： Chest X-ray, brain MRI, and fundus photography - covering diverse clinical scenarios. 胸部 X 光、脑 MRI 和眼底照相 - 覆盖多样的临床场景。
  • Medical Quality Control:医学质量控制： LLM-as-Judge with specialized medical rubric ensures anatomical accuracy and clinical plausibility. LLM-as-Judge 配合专业医学评分准则确保解剖准确性和临床合理性。

Quality Control质量控制

LLM-as-Judge evaluation results showing pass rates across different quality criteria and modalities. LLM-as-Judge 评估结果，显示不同质量标准和模态的通过率。

Our Quality Control Process:

  • Medically Grounded Rubric: Evaluates instruction compliance, structural plausibility, realism, and fidelity preservation.
  • History-Aware Refinement: Iteratively improves edits based on previous feedback to enhance quality.
  • Multi-Round Evaluation: Ensures consistent quality across all generated images.

我们的质量控制流程：

  • 医学评分准则： 评估指令依从性、结构合理性、真实性和保真度。
  • 历史感知优化： 基于先前反馈迭代改进编辑以提高质量。
  • 多轮评估： 确保所有生成图像的一致质量。

Distribution of refinement rounds needed to achieve high-quality edits, demonstrating the effectiveness of iterative improvement. 达到高质量编辑所需的优化轮数分布，展示迭代改进的有效性。
